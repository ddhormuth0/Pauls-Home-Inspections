Services

It is my job to give you an unbiased, professional opinion of the condition of the structure, it's components, and their overall functionality. This is provided in a well organized, professional electronic report with pictures, comments, and diagrams, explaining my observations and opinions. The overall goal of my inspection is to ensure that your expectations are appropriate with the house you are proposing to buy or sell. Once the client has a copy of the inspection, I will be available by appointment withing the next seven (7) days to explain my findings and observations. I find it beneficial to go over the inspection with the client to resolve any questions about the process. The inspection report will be emailed to the client within 24 hours of the inspection. Any re-inspection is included in the original price but will be limited to any repair work completed.
